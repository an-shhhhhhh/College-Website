CORE VALUES

VISION:- Create high-quality engineering professionals through research, innovation and teamwork for a lasting technology development in the area of Electronics and Communication Engineering.

MISSION :- To offer a well-balanced programme of instruction, lab practices, research & development activities, product incubation. To provide a superior learning experience with a vision of the future educational needs of society. Provide ethical and value based education by promoting activities addressing the societal needs. Enable students to develop skills to solve complex technological problems of current times and also provide a framework for promoting collaborative and multidisciplinary activities.

OBJECTIVE OF THE ELECTRONIC & TELECOMMUNICATIONENGINEERING ENGINEER PROGRAM

The objectives of the ELECTRONIC & TELECOMMUNICATION ENGINEERING program at the CCOEW are to prepare graduates for professional practice and life-long learning.

An ability to design and conduct experiments as well as to analyze and interpret data. An ability to use the techniques, skills, and modern engineering tools necessary for electronics and Telecommunication engineering practice. Having strong ethical and professional responsibility and adhering to quality.
Be receptive to new technologies and attain professional competence through lifelong learning such as advanced degrees, professional registration, publications and other professional activities.

Program Specific Outcomes

Problem analysis: Identify, formulate, review research literature, and analyze complex engineering problems reaching substantiated conclusions using first principles of mathematics, natural sciences, and engineering sciences.

Modern tool usage: Create, select, and apply appropriate techniques, resources, and modern engineering and IT tools including prediction and modeling to complex engineering activities with an understanding of the limitations.

The engineer and society: Apply reasoning informed by the contextual knowledge to assess societal, health, safety, legal and cultural issues and the consequent responsibilities relevant to the professional engineering practice.

CAREER PERSPECTIVE


Engineer for R&D Software:

The Research and Development Software Engineer develops, designs, and tests new software system products. Add-ons include knowledge of mechanical and process controls, technical skills like automation, and so on. When it comes to software and electronics, all of India’s major industries have a tendency to focus on their overall research and development at every stage. This profile opens up a very wide range of business options, including the most well-known ones!

Sales Manager:

The fundamental responsibilities of a sales manager in electronics and communication engineering (ECE) include growth and sales by achieving targets, product sales, and team management.

Nanotechnology:

Recently, a lot of industries are using nanotechnology to make their products, like solar cells, tiny sensors, tracking chips, or transistors, smaller but more effective. Additionally, it is evident that a growing number of diverse industries will turn to nanotechnology over time. Thus, the best electronics and telecommunication engineering colleges in Coimbatore assists the students to adapt to these new techs for their future.

Career in Automation and Control Systems:

Nowadays, a lot of businesses are incorporating robots and automation into a variety of fields to improve overall operation proficiency. There is a golden opportunity for anyone with an interest in robotics who comes from an ECE background, and it is evident that the demand is constantly increasing.
