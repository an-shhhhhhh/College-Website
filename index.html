Welcome To Cummins College Of Engineering For Women

Cummins College of Engineering for Women is the top Engineering College for Women in Nagpur, & becoming the Best Engineering College in Nagpur.

Cummins College of Engineering for Women at Nagpur Established on 27th Feb 2010 & started with funding of Rs. 8 Crores from Cummins India Foundation specifically to empower women of Vidarbha region of Maharashtra.Our Motto is Sharpening Engineering Acumen of women with a Difference. “To develop women professionals who are academically and technically sound with strong work ethics and above all good human beings”, is the mission of Cummins College of Engineering for women, Nagpur. The college is approved by AICTE, New Delhi, and Government of Maharashtra, affiliated to RTM Nagpur University. The College has Management Leadership of Shri Milindji Kukde who is retired Execuitive Engineer from MSEB and has dynamic leadership of Dr. Bharatbhushan Joshi who is Ex-DRDO scientist having 27 years of experience in R&D, Industry & Academics. The excellent infrastructure, talented & well qualified faculty and student centric approach has made Cummins College of Engineering, Nagpur as the most preferred girls’ college in Nagpur.

Vision

Empowerment and enrichment of women professionals through education; innovative training of life skills and job skills; for pioneering the entry of women in the latest technological areas and job domains; encouraging self-employment and sensitizing awareness for women’s issue in the society.

Mission

To develop technically and academically sound women professionals, having strong work-ethics and good human-being characteristics.

Core Values

* Enhance professionalism with good human values.
* Promote team spirit and healthy competition.
* Create healthy atmosphere for effective teaching–learning process.
* Value individual differences and dignity of labor.
* Sharing of experience, knowledge and skills.
