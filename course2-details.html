MECHANICAL ENGINEERING

Mechanical engineering really appeals to those people who like hands-on work and who like to see tangible results. We get to take some courses that were really interesting because they were a bit of all sorts of engineering disciplines. The good thing about mechanical engineering is it’s so versatile. It applies to so many different industries.​

ABOUT

Mechanical engineering uses the principles of physics and materials science for the analysis and design of mechanical and thermal systems.Mechanical Engineering covers a range of subtopics including mechatronics and robotics, structural analysis, thermodynamics and engineering design.Mechanical engineers research, design, test, develop and manufacture various mechanical devices. The work of mechanical engineers surrounds us in our everyday lives, which means the techniques and skills you’ll gain will be directly applicable to a wide range of professional environments.

CORE VALUES

VISION:- To be a world leader in, and renowned for, mechanical engineering and research.

Our graduates will embrace professional development and lifelong learning relevant to their careers.

MISSION :- Our mission is to educate girls to become mechanical engineers and visioneers who are innovative, entrepreneurial, and equipped to become global leaders in research and technology.

To impart quality education to create efficient Mechanical Engineers who can compete at the global level.
To build State -of – the – art research facilities in Mechanical Engineering in order to enable the faculty and students to learn and disseminate knowledge and innovate in their applications.

OBJECTIVE OF THE MECHANICAL ENGINEER PROGRAM

The objectives of the MECHANICAL ERNGINEER program at the CCOEW is : To enable students to have fundamental understanding of the core concepts of Mechanical Engineering

To integrate analytical and computational ability with experimental skills to create individuals competent in professional engineering practices.

An ability to design a system, component, or process to meet desired needs within realistic constraints such as economic, environmental, social, political, ethical, health and safety, manufacturability, and sustainability

PROGRAM SPECIFIC OUTCOMES

Applying the fundamental knowledge of Mechanical engineering in the fields of Thermal, Design, Manufacturing/ Production and industrial engineering.

Communicating effectively to work as a team member and/or leader with professional ethics

Problem Analysis: Identify, formulate, review research literature, and analyze complex engineering problems reaching substantiated conclusions using first principles of mathematics, natural sciences and engineering sciences.

Project Management and Finance: Demonstrate knowledge and understanding of the engineering and management principles and apply these to one's own work, as a member and leader in a team, to manage projects and in multidisciplinary environments.

CAREER PERSPECTIVE


Automotive Engineer

Automation Engineering has expanded into a full-fledged domain in itself with the introduction of advanced computerised tools and techniques and the need to make vehicles adaptable to human comfort levels. After studying Mechanical Engineering, you can opt to work in this field in the areas of design, production and development of vehicles and handling responsibilities of improving systems of exhaust, hydraulics, aerodynamics, and emissions as well as take care of financial and cost management.

Manufacturing Systems Engineer

The process and systems of manufacturing across several industries offer individuals a wide variety of opportunities to make a career in Mechanical Engineering. Common tasks may include, overseeing quality of raw materials, efficient production lines, wastage of materials and a host of other responsibilities.

Engineering Consultant

Typically considered a senior role in Management, engineering consultants are in charge of large scale projects which involve heavy use of both core engineering and managerial skills. They regularly liaison with designers, health and safety experts, manufacturers, production engineers and quality engineers to ensure timely and satisfactory completion of projects. An MBA in Project Management is highly recommended for those wanting to opt for this career profile after Mechanical Engineering.

Nuclear Engineer

Serving as a nuclear engineer, you will work in designing and maintenance of nuclear power plants in order to maintain efficiency and stability. In addition to that, tasks may include overseeing compliance with health, and safety regulatory standards, and solving operational problems, and environmental concerns, among others. Nuclear engineers also frequently liaison with govt. officials and power management professionals to ensure the proper functioning of nuclear power plants.
