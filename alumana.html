An instituion of higher education is a partnership among students and alumni, faculty and administration, donors and trustees, neighbourhoods and more , to build a community- and a culture.

Our Alumni

An Alumnae association is an association of graduates or, more broadly, of former students (Alumnae). These associations often organizes social events, publishes newsletters or magazines, guides the passed out students, provide opportunities and raise funds for the organization. Alumnae Association of Cummins College of Engineering for Women, Nagpur has come into effect from 2014.
